How to give feedback

Click Send Feedback on any class card. A short window will open with a prompt: "What are your thoughts on this class...". Type your message and click Send.

Tips
  • Be specific: mention the trainer or session time if relevant.
  • Be constructive: suggestions help improve future classes.
  • Your feedback is anonymous to other members.
