Our Combative Classes

We provide high quality training for anyone who is willing to take the challenge.

  • Boxing Class

    Boxing Class: The Striking Revolution

    Baguio City sits at an elevation of over 5,000 feet, making its thin mountain air the ultimate natural forge for cardiovascular endurance. It is no secret why eight division world champion Manny Pacquiao chose these very mountains for his most grueling training camps to build his legendary stamina. At GOALS Gym Baguio, we channel that historic legacy alongside the unbreakable grit of Filipino icons like Flash Elorde, Nonito Donaire, and Donnie Nietes. In our classes, you will dive deep into the sweet science. You will learn the biomechanics behind generating knockout power from the ground up, how to utilize angles to slip attacks, and the footwork required to command the ring. Whether your goal is to build iron clad lungs, learn elite self defense, or master the chess match of striking, our boxing program will turn your hands into serious weapons.

  • Kick Boxing Class

    Image Credit - ONE FC

    Kick Boxing Class: Where East Meets West

    Kickboxing is a dynamic combat sport that merges the crisp footwork and punching mechanics of traditional western boxing with the powerful kicks of martial arts like Karate and Taekwondo. The sport was born in the 1950s when Japanese martial artists wanted to test their skills in full contact bouts, eventually exploding in global popularity during the legendary K1 kickboxing era. Today, the sport is thriving in the Philippines, and the Cordilleras are the absolute epicenter of that striking revolution. We take massive pride in local highland warriors like Jean Claude Saclag, a multi gold medalist at the Southeast Asian Games who showcased the immense striking power of our region to the world. At GOALS Gym Baguio, you will learn how to seamlessly chain boxing combinations into lightning fast head and body kicks. It is the perfect discipline for anyone looking to build explosive speed, elite cardiovascular endurance, and practical self defense skills without having to worry about takedowns or ground fighting.

  • Muay Thai Class

    Muay Thai Class: The Art of Eight Limbs

    Known globally as the Art of Eight Limbs, Muay Thai transforms your fists, elbows, knees, and shins into a devastating arsenal. The sport is steeped in ancient traditions, like the Wai Kru Ram Muay dance of respect, and the legendary shin conditioning that allows fighters to literally kick through baseball bats. Here in Baguio City, the striking arts run deep in our blood. We are incredibly proud of homegrown Cordilleran athletes like Islay Erika Bomogao, who showcased our local striking power to the world by becoming an IFMA World Champion. In our classes, we mix that fierce local fighting spirit with the brutal, highly technical systems of global legends like Buakaw Banchamek. You will master thunderous roundhouse kicks, tactical clinch work that dictates the pace, and the elite conditioning that makes Thai fighters the most feared strikers on earth.

  • Adult MMA Class

    Image Credit - ONE FC

    Adult MMA: The Complete Combat System

    Mixed Martial Arts is the ultimate proving ground for human combat. While the concept traces its ancient roots back to the brutal Greek Olympic games of Pankration, the modern sport exploded into mainstream consciousness in the 1990s with the famous rise of the UFC. The early days of the Ultimate Fighting Championship were created to answer one simple question about which martial art works best in a real fight. The world quickly learned that a true fighter must master them all. MMA fuses the devastating striking of Boxing and Kickboxing, the relentless takedowns of Wrestling, and the chess like ground control of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu. The Philippines, and specifically our home in the Cordilleras, has produced some of the most feared MMA warriors on the planet. Incredible local athletes like Eduard Folayang, Joshua Pacio, Kevin Belingon, and Danny Kingad have captured world titles and dominated the ONE Championship cage, proving that the mountain warrior spirit is truly world class. At GOALS Gym Baguio, our Adult MMA program teaches you this complete and battle tested system. You will learn how to strike with knockout power, transition seamlessly into takedowns, and control or finish fights on the mat.

  • Kids MMA Class

    Kids MMA: Building Little Champions (Ages 11 and below)

    Every great martial artist starts somewhere, and science shows that combat sports drastically improve a child's focus, emotional regulation, and physical coordination. At GOALS Gym Baguio, our Kids MMA program molds the next generation by blending basic striking, grappling, and practical self defense into highly engaging lessons. We ground our teachings in deep rooted Filipino values of respect and discipline. Beyond just learning how to throw a proper punch or secure a takedown safely, this training instills a true champion mindset. Your kids will learn perseverance when facing challenges, respect for their coaches and training partners, and the quiet, bully proof confidence to stand up for themselves. Watch them build functional habits that will set them up for a lifetime of success.

  • Brazilian Jui Jitsu Class

    Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu: The Gentle Art That Conquered the World (MWF, Sat)

    Brazilian Jiu Jitsu completely revolutionized the martial arts world by proving that skeletal structure and leverage can neutralize sheer size and brute strength. The secret lies in taking the fight to the ground and utilizing the "guard" position, turning your legs into a defensive and offensive shield. It is physical chess played at lightning speed. The BJJ scene in the Philippines is exploding right now, driven by world class homegrown champions like Meggie Ochoa and Annie Ramirez who have firmly put our country on the global grappling map. At GOALS Gym Baguio, you will learn how to bypass an opponent's defenses, control their center of gravity, and apply precise joint locks and chokes. BJJ builds incredible core strength, sharpens your split second problem solving skills under pressure, and proves that technical intelligence always beats physical intimidation.

  • Wushu Taolu Class

    Wushu Taolu: The Heritage of Mountain Warriors

    The Cordilleras are rightfully recognized as the combat sports capital of the Philippines, and much of that fearsome reputation was built on the foundation of Wushu. Legendary martial artists from Baguio and Benguet, such as Eduard Folayang, Joshua Pacio, and Danny Kingad, began their phenomenal combat journeys competing in Wushu Sanda. Sanda is the gritty, full contact sparring side of Wushu that combines brutal kickboxing with lightning fast, high amplitude takedowns that score massive points. On the flip side is Wushu Taolu, the spectacular performance based art. Taolu involves highly choreographed forms, gravity defying aerial kicks, and flowing movements that require extreme flexibility and balance. At GOALS Gym Baguio, our program honors this deeply rooted local heritage. Whether you are drawn to the explosive striking power of our local MMA heroes or the beautiful cinematic agility of Taolu, you will forge the iron focus and athleticism that built the champions of our mountains.
