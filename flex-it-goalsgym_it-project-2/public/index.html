Boxing Class

Improve your punches, footwork, and overall conditioning through our boxing program. At GOALS Gym Baguio, we focus on proper technique and discipline while giving you a workout that builds confidence, strength, and stamina perfect for beginners and hobbyists in Baguio.

Kick Boxing Class

Experience the “Art of Eight Limbs” with our Muay Thai training. At GOALS Gym Baguio, we teach real striking techniques using punches, kicks, elbows, and knees. This class helps improve balance, power, and confidence swak para sa mga gustong matuto ng totoong striking.

Muay Thai Class

Muay Thai is a combat sport from Thailand that uses eight points of contact—fists, elbows, knees, and shins and is known as "The Art of Eight Limbs" for its comprehensive striking system.

Adult MMA Class

Our Adult MMA class combines striking, grappling, and ground fighting. At GOALS Gym Baguio, you’ll train in a supportive environment while building strength, flexibility, and self-defense skills. Whether fitness or competition ang goal mo, this class is perfect for adults in Baguio.

Kids MMA Class (11 years old and below)

Kids MMA at GOALS Gym Baguio helps children build discipline, confidence, and coordination through fun and safe martial arts training. This class teaches basic skills while developing respect, focus, and healthy habits for kids.

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Class (MWF, Sat)

Learn Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu a martial art focused on technique, leverage, and ground control. At GOALS Gym Baguio, this class helps improve problem-solving, strength, and self-defense awareness. Perfect for anyone looking for a practical and mentally engaging martial art here in Baguio.
